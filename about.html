About Medimize, Inc.

Medimize, Inc. is an innovative healthcare technology start-up with a mission to enable better patient care by providing physicians with wider access to select clinical data

​

The company was founded on the belief that the best clinical decisions are made from informed decisions. Medimize's unique data model and HIPPA compliant, secure technology platform aggregates and indexes select clinical data from various, permissioned sources and serves it up to a physician in a specific way to:

​
  • Maximize physician productivity
  • Minimize unnecessary medical testing and admissions
  • Drive better patient outcomes

Medimize is an easy to use on-line reference solution that clinicians can use, in addition to their hospital's Electronic Medical/Health Records system, to access a wider set of clinical data
