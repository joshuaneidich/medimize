Affiliations

As an innovative HealthTech start-up, Medimize is building key relationships with leading organizations in the healthcare and start-up ecosystem:

Medimize is a proud member of Pennovation Works. Developed by the University of Pennsylvania, Pennovation Works is a business incubator that provides office and lab space to drive innovation and foster entrepreneurial initiatives.

Medimize is a proud member of the Philadelphia Alliance for Capital and Technologies (PACT). PACT's mission is to drive innovation and growth for businesses and entrepreneurs in the Philadelphia region.

​

Medimize is a proud member of the Mentor Connect program. The program leverages the wisdom and insight of the Ben Franklin Technology Partners and PACT communities for the progress of their entrepreneurs, Mentor Connect brings the experience of a curated team of leaders and experts to the high-potential entrepreneurs most ready to grow, assisting development of the business skills they need to bring ideas and inventions to market.
